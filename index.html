Why walk, if you can get DronQ?

With the ease of a button press, you can get an ice cold drink without getting up from your comfy seat.

DronQ

DronQ Industries is a company that is focussed on the use of drones. With the ease of a button press, you can get an ice-cold drink without getting up from your comfy seat. You don’t have to stand up to have a drink. Just open the app on your phone and select the drink you want.

Easy delivery

DronQ flies any kind of canned drink inside and around the house. This takes only a minute from selecting to delivery! You don’t have to stand up to have a drink. Just take your phone and select the drink you want. DronQ will fly to you and put it right in front of you.

Order from your comfy seat

DronQ can be used be used as a really cool gadget, but also when you don’t like of just can’t walk. For example, when you’re ill or having difficulty walking.

DronQ Station

With the drone, you also get a compact fridge. This can be filled with your choice of 33cl cans of soda. There is place for 18 cans which stay on the perfect temperature on a hot summer day. Also, the DronQ charges on top of the Station. After 15 minutes of charging it can fly 15 minutes to deliver your drinks.

Easy use with the app

An easy to use application with all the necessary information. One-click drink delivery, live DronQ Station stock and live DronQ power statistics. With the help of your phone signal, both optical and radio, your DronQ will always find you around the house. Once your DronQ finds you, it will look for your signal light on your phone. It knows where to put your fresh and cold beverage.

Safety for all

Safety is our number one priority. With specially designed propeller guards and battery management the DronQ will be the safest drone around. Because of all the safety precautions it can be managed and used by all ages. The DronQ will be save around all kinds of animals without causing them any harm. Also, with many sensors, the DronQ will fly around any obstacle.
